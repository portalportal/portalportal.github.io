App Privacy Policy

  End users personal info

This app does not collect or transmit any end user's personally identifiable information (like First Name, Last Name, Age, Genre etc.). No personal information is used, stored, secured or disclosed by services this app works with.

  Logged in users personal info

During login this app does not collect or transmit the end user's YouTube© user name and password login info and any other critical inofrmation related with the end user.

The login occurs in the YouTube© authorization page hosted in a web browser externally from the app.

After login the app regarding the business part may view and manage your videos, playlists and activity and in future releases may view your contacts related with your YouTube© account.

In order to enable the advanced features of the app, during the login process and after the Sign In, the end user must allow access to the app with one of the names a] "Your Tube 8" b] "Your Tube 8 (V3)" to view and manage his videos, playlists and activity.

  Technical information

When you are using this app and submit data, you are creating a query request which is forwarded to YouTube©.

YouTube© replies to you through an RSS or a Json Feed for viewing the content.

Limited technical information is sent (such as your IP address included in the HTTP calls the app makes) and may be used from YouTube© for usability and statistical reasons.

When you are using this app for video download, you are creating a download URI which is forwarded to the current available servers and your download starts.

Technical information is sent (such as your IP address and signature, access token the URI needs to be valid, included in the HTTP calls the app makes) and may be used from YouTube© mainly for usability and statistical reasons and in case of any reported violation for legal reasons.

In future releases your watch history videos will be stored in a folder the app is going to create for first time with name Your_Tube_8_Pro. The folder is going to be created inside your Videos Library folder. The data will be stored in an xml file with name Your_Tube_8_History.xml. This file must not be modified away from the app because it is possible the app to do not work properly.

  Content Share

This app allows you to share content with Windows store Apps characterized as shared targets.

Sample shared targets are the Winodows 8 Mail and the People app.

When you share content from this app you are always able to review and change the default content we send to your shared target app before to submit.

  Statistical information

A small set of non-identifiable information may be sent to Microsoft© for statistical reasons about app usage, device type and capabilities.

  Third parties

If in the future (this app next versions) the app will make use of other services none reported in this privacy policy, their usage of information is excluded from this privacy policy. You will be clearly made aware of the involved services in the app and we will ensure the very minimal set of data is sent to those services.

This app is supported by ads from Microsoft Advertising. Learn more about Microsoft’s privacy practices and your choices. Learn more link: https://choice.microsoft.com/AdvertisementChoice/.
